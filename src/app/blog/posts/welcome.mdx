## Sorry, I'm not here right now. Gone Severless. 
### (I'm everywhere.)

Welcome to my online portfolio, resume, and blog created using Once-UI. I decided to take this project serverless and go live on the edge somewhere. 
This website is hosted using Cloudflare pages, and from my past experience you aren't going to find a faster way to serve your site than it residing 
on a CDN. 

I really should have setup a portfolio sooner, but I was never really happy with the various frameworks until I found Once-UI. 
I'm in the process of migrating content from an older site of mine to this one, so stay tuned! I got a lot more content coming when time permits. You can expect a few 
technical tutorials, cybersecurity rants, programming guides, and lots of misc. stuff.
